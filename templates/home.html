INCORRECT POSTURE

Incorrect posture during exercise can result in increased risk of injury due to undue strain on muscles and joints. It can also compromise the effectiveness of the exercise by engaging the wrong muscles or limiting range of motion. Consistently maintaining proper posture ensures safer and more efficient workouts.
